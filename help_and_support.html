Troubleshooting Missing Grades

If you are experiencing issues with missing grades on our platform, please follow these steps to troubleshoot and resolve the problem:

1- Check Submission Status

Ensure that you have successfully submitted your assignments or assessments. Review your submission history in your user account to confirm.

2- Refresh or Reload the Page

Sometimes, missing information can be resolved by refreshing or reloading the page. Use the refresh button on your browser or press Ctrl + R (Windows) or Cmd + R (Mac) to reload.

3- Clear Browser Cache

Clearing your browser cache and cookies can help resolve display issues. Follow the instructions for your specific browser to clear the cache.

4- Verify Account Information

Double-check that the account information associated with your profile is accurate. Ensure your username, email address, and other details are up-to-date.

5- Contact The Instructor or Administrator

If the issue persists, reach out to your instructor or system administrator for assistance. They may provide insights into any system-wide issues or offer guidance specific to your cours
